How to Initiate a Video Call

Prerequisite You should have already been the guest on a video call
Prerequisite You should already have the TelePatriot app
Prerequisite You should see "Video Chat" in the TelePatriot menu. If you don't see "Video Chat", contact your state leadership and ask for this permission.
Step 1 Get your guest's mobile number. Agree on a time to call.
Step 2 Confirm the guest's HD and SD. If not known, check OpenStates.org
Step 3 Look up the legislator on this page. Confirm that TelePatriot has the correct legislator for the chosen district.
Step 4 Confirm that email address and social media handles are present/correct. If not, add/update them on this page. Focus on Facebook and Twitter handles. If they aren't listed, try to find them.
Step 5 Know the representative's position on COS. It's much more powerful if you mention their position on video
